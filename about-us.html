About Regnant Properties & Investment Ltd.

Regnant Properties & Investment is a dedicated and committed real estate company that has the core-objective of satisfying customers by offering litigation-free assets at an affordable cost. We started operation in 2016. We look forward to shaping and creating significant progress in both the real estate and construction industry of Ghana.

Our team maintains the highest standards of management and workmanship. Projects are staffed with the most experienced and highly qualified construction experts by making use of professionals and reliable subcontractors whose experience meets the demands of the projects they are requested to work on.

Our loyalty to our customers is unquestionable; we execute and deliver diligently assets just as it is requested regardless of the magnitude and size.
